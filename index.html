About HostelFix

HostelFix is designed to bridge the communication gap between hostel residents and administrators. We understand that a comfortable living environment is crucial for academic success and well-being. That's why we've built an intuitive platform where students can easily log complaints, track their status, and receive timely resolutions.

For administrators, HostelFix provides a centralized dashboard to manage incoming complaints, assign tasks, monitor progress, and ensure accountability. Our goal is to create a more responsive and efficient hostel management system for everyone.
